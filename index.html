We all live on OneMoreMeal™

OneMoreMeal provides a community-based data solution for local institutions that, in the short term, solves local hunger, and, in the long term, helps social welfare organizations improve poverty condition though insights and data.

OneMoreMeal is an initiative for helping the homeless through common folks' power. You can make your share of contribution through purchasing at McDonald's!
